This document contains all the features of Arc.

## 1. Organization & Navigation
*   **Spaces:** Separate environments for different contexts (e.g., Work, Personal, Side Projects) to keep tabs and settings distinct.
*   **Profiles:** Completely separate browser data (cookies, history, logins) that can be assigned to specific Spaces.
*   **Pinned Tabs & Favorites:** Pinned tabs stay at the top of your sidebar. "Favorites" are top-level pinned tabs (circular icons) that persist across all Spaces within a Profile.
*   **Folders:** Organize your pinned tabs into groups for better management.
*   **Sidebar:** A collapsible, vertical navigation hub that replaces the traditional top tab bar.
*   **Air Traffic Control:** A powerful routing tool that automatically opens specific URLs in designated Spaces or Profiles.
*   **Auto Archive:** Automatically cleans up "Today's Tabs" after a set period of inactivity (e.g., 12 hours) to prevent clutter.
*   **Command Bar (Ctrl+T / Ctrl+L):** A central search and action hub to find tabs, history, and execute browser commands quickly.

## 2. Multitasking & Efficiency
*   **Split View:** View up to 4 tabs simultaneously in a single window, either horizontally or vertically.
*   **Peek:** Instantly preview links (like search results or calendar invites) in a pop-up window without leaving your current page.
*   **Little Arc:** A lightweight, separate browser window that opens when you click links from external apps (like Slack or Email), keeping your main browser focused.
*   **Mini Player:** A floating video player that continues playing in the corner of your screen when you switch tabs.
*   **Live Previews:** Hover over pinned tabs for specific sites (like Google Calendar, Outlook, or GitHub) to see live updates and notifications without clicking.

## 3. Arc Max (AI-Powered Features)
*   **Tidy Tabs:** Automatically organizes and renames tabs into logical groups.
*   **Tidy Downloads:** Renames downloaded files to be more descriptive and easier to find later.
*   **5-Second Previews:** Summarizes a webpage's content when you hover over a link and press Shift.
*   **Ask on Page:** Use AI to search for and summarize information within the current page (integrated into Ctrl+F).

## 4. Personalization & Creativity
*   **Themes (Paints):** Highly customizable UI colors, gradients, and textures (though some advanced features like grain and system themes are currently macOS-only).
*   **Boosts:** Customize the look and feel of any website using CSS and JavaScript (e.g., changing colors or hiding annoying elements).
*   **Easels:** A flexible digital canvas for collecting images, text, and "live" snippets of websites that update in real-time.
*   **Capture:** A built-in screenshot tool that can snap specific elements or entire pages.
*   **Developer Mode:** A specialized UI for web developers with quick access to inspect elements and developer tools.

## 5. Smart & Connected Features
*   **Live Calendars:** View upcoming meetings and join them directly from the sidebar.
*   **GitHub Live Folders:** An automatic folder that tracks your open Pull Requests and notifications.
*   **Handoff:** Seamlessly transition tabs between your desktop and the Arc Search mobile app.
